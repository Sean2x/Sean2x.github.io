About Us

Welcome to the cutting edge of creation! We’re a retrofuturistic collective of 3D printing, electronics, and robotics enthusiasts bringing tomorrow’s ideas to life today. From mechatronics to design innovation, we empower members to push boundaries and turn concepts into reality.

Current Project

We’re building an autonomous rover using ESP32 controllers, brushless motors, and custom-printed chassis parts. The goal? Navigate complex environments with precision—and look good doing it.

Past Projects

  • Saturn V Rocket Model with motion and lighting
  • LED Interactive Wall Art for student showcases
  • Custom prosthetics designed for local outreach
  • Fully 3D-printed mechanical keyboard builds
