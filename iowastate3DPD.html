ABOUT OUR CLUB

Welcome to 3DPD

Founded in 2020, 3DPD is a hands-on community of makers, engineers, and designers at Iowa State passionate about exploring the frontiers of robotics, electronics, and 3D printing.

Our club combines the nostalgia of 80s sci-fi visions with cutting-edge additive manufacturing techniques to create unique, functional, and artistic pieces that bridge past and future.

Our Mission

  • Push the boundaries of engineering by combining creativity, technology, and design
  • Build a collaborative space where learning, innovation, and experimentation thrive
  • Inspire the next generation of engineers to turn bold ideas into real-world solutions
